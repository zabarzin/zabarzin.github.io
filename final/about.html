HELLO, MY NAME IS ZAHRA BARZIN!

This past May, I graduated with a Degree in Graphic Design, at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. I am currently living in the lively city of Boston, but am originally from the island of Jamestown in Rhode Island.

While I do enjoy print design, I am especially fascinated by both the research and creative design possibilities that come with UI/UX Design. And I am excited to say that I am advancing my HTML/CSS skills as well. As a designer I strongly stand by being conceptually relevant through all of my work. And with that in mind, I am am always self driven to push design concepts. Outside of my profession as a graphic designer, I enjoy the ocean, reading, traveling and writing!
